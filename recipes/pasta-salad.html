Odin's Pasta Salad

This delicious pasta salad filled with vitamins and proteins will give you energy for days!!

Deep in the constrains of Asgard, only one thing can make Odin feel better after a night out drinking with the Valkyries... THIS PASTA SALAD!!

And now, you too can learn the secret to eternal fulfilment. Feast your eyes and your soul, for we are about to give you the secret to this timeless recipe. Here is what you will need to accomplish this delight for your mortal palates:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 bottle of Italian-style salad dressing
  • 6 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can of black olives, chopped

Preparation

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Boil water and cook the pasta
  3. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth. Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers and olives in a salad bowl.
  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat
  5. Refrigerate salad, 8 hours to overnight
  6. FEAST!!!
