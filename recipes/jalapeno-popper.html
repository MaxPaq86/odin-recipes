Odin's mighty jalapeno poppers

One bite of these poppers will be enough to convince you!

I mean, just look at those things. Jalapeno... wrapped... in... bacon... Nuff' said!

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Jalapenos
  • Bacon

Preparation

  1. Make the filling
  2. Fill each jalapeno half with cheese mixture, then put the halves together
  3. Wrap each reassembled jalapeno with bacon
  4. Cook in the preheated oven until the bacon is crispy
